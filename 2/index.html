Name:

Nathan Simon

Descriptor:

Nathan Simon is a engineer with NASA, and is interested in investing in our company.

Quote:

I believe in the future of an independent and prosperous space agency, free from the burden of bureaucracy.

Who is it?:

Mr. Simon is a 36 year old engineer currently employed by NASA. He currently lives in the Houston, TX area, but is willing to move to work with Outer State.

What Goals?:

Mr. Simon is dedicated to the idea that one day, private industry will be able to take over the space transportation market, and leave government run agencies to other projects.

What Attitude?:

Luckily for us, he is eager to help support the growing market of space transportation and is focused on making sure it becomes a sustained market for years to come.

What Behavior?:

Organized, likes efficiency, and not a fan of messy/confused design layouts. If we want to land him, we must make the website easy to use and make the message standout. He’s easily distracted when using websites, which is a damn shame considering he’s a dedicated engineer.
